General description of VOXi website and services

The VOXi website allows users to post their bucket list, life aspirations, goals, and dreams online. Images, descriptions, and updates can be added to these items at any time. Future functionality and services will evolve over time. Generically, we will refer to these posts as “dreams.” The functionality of the site is the social interaction among users sharing dreams.

Authentication and commenting services are provided by Facebook.

When dreams have been created, updated, or completed you will be prompted to share this with Facebook. When you agree to sharing with Facebook, VOXi will create a post on your Facebook wall describing the activity and title of the dream along with a link back to your dream page.

How we use cookies

When you access our website, we, or companies we hire to track how our website is used, may place small data files called "cookies" on your computer. We and our service providers also use cookies to customize the VOXi Services, content and advertising; measure promotional effectiveness, and promote trust and safety.

We send a "session cookie" to your computer when you log in to your account or otherwise use VOXi. This type of cookie helps us to recognize you if you visit multiple pages on our site during the same session, so that we don’t need to ask you for your password on each page. We do not expire sessions on window close, you can end a session by clicking the log out link that is visible on all pages when a session is set. Once you log out or close your browser, this cookie expires and no longer has any effect.

We encode our cookies so that only we can interpret the information stored in them. You are free to decline our cookies if your browser permits, but doing so may interfere with your use of our website. We may also collect information about your computer or other access device to mitigate risk and for fraud prevention purposes.

You may encounter cookies from third parties such as Facebook and third-party websites or service providers linking to VOXi when using VOXi on websites that we do not control (for example, if you view a web page created by a third party or use an application developed by a third party, there may be a cookie placed by that web page or application.)
